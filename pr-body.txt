💡 What: Replaced `Array.find` checks with a `Set` for symbol existence tracking during extraction.
🎯 Why: Fixes an O(N^2) complexity bottleneck inside a loop, greatly improving performance for large files.
📊 Impact: Reduced large file symbol extraction from ~42s down to ~216ms.
🔬 Measurement: Benchmark with 80,000 definitions showed a 196x speedup (42476.99 ms vs 216.33 ms).
